Guided morning treks

Begin your day with a peaceful guided trek through scenic trails as the morning sun rises. Enjoy fresh air, natural beauty, and insights from local guides for a refreshing and energizing experience.

Swimming Pool

Relax and unwind in our well-maintained swimming pool, surrounded by calm natural views. Whether for a refreshing dip or leisurely swim, it’s the perfect place to cool off and enjoy your stay.

Zipline

Experience the thrill of gliding above lush landscapes on our zipline adventure. Enjoy breathtaking views and an exciting ride that adds a touch of adrenaline to your stay.

Sky Cycling

Pedal high above the ground on a secure sky cycling track and enjoy stunning views all around. A fun and thrilling activity that blends adventure with scenic beauty.

carrom

Enjoy a classic game of carrom in a relaxed setting, perfect for family and friends. A fun indoor activity that adds leisure and friendly competition to your stay.

Table Tennis

Challenge your friends or family to a lively game of table tennis. A fun and energetic indoor activity suitable for all age groups.

Rope Activities

Test your balance and strength with exciting rope-based activities set in a safe outdoor environment. A perfect mix of fun, challenge, and adventure for all age groups.

Bond Fire

Gather around a warm bonfire under the open sky and enjoy a relaxing evening with friends and family. A perfect setting for conversations, laughter, and memorable moments.

Volleyball

Enjoy a fun and active game of volleyball in an open outdoor setting. Perfect for group play, team bonding, and adding energy to your resort experience.

Cricket Play Area

Spend quality time playing cricket in our open play area, ideal for friendly matches and group fun. A great way to stay active and enjoy the outdoors during your stay.

Shuttlecock

Enjoy a light and refreshing game of shuttlecock in a relaxed outdoor space. Perfect for casual play, family fun, and friendly competition.
