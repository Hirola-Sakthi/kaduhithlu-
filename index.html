FAQ

Everything You Need to Know About Staying With Us

What are the check-in and check-out timings?

Check-in time is 12:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability.

What types of accommodation are available?

We offer spacious cottages suitable for double, triple, and quadruple sharing, designed for comfort and privacy.

Are meals included in the stay?

Yes, we serve simple and delicious home-style meals. Meal plans can be confirmed at the time of booking.

Does the resort have a swimming pool?

Yes, we have an outdoor natural pool where guests can relax and enjoy the scenic surroundings.

What activities are available at the resort?

Guests can enjoy indoor and outdoor games like cricket, volleyball, badminton, chess, carrom, trekking, stream walking, and evening campfires.

Is the resort suitable for families and groups?

Absolutely. Kaduhithlu Resort is ideal for families, couples, and group getaways.

How far is the resort from Sakleshpur town?

The resort is approximately 28 km from Sakleshpur Railway Station and easily accessible by road.

Is parking available at the property?

Yes, we provide secure parking facilities for our guests.
