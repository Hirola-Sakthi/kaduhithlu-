About Our Resort

Where Nature Meets Serenity

From refreshing mornings to quiet evenings under the stars, every moment here is crafted to leave you feeling renewed and refreshed.

Our Nature Retreat

Comfortable Stays & Meaningful Experiences

At Kaduhithlu Resort, we offer thoughtfully designed cottages perfect for couples, families, and groups, blending naturally with the surrounding coffee plantations while providing modern comfort. Our rooms embrace a simple, peaceful lifestyle that allows guests to truly relax and feel at home. Beyond your stay, enjoy serene plantation walks, refreshing treks, and stream explorations, and as evening sets in, gather around our warm campfires where stories are shared and unforgettable memories are created.

FAQ

Everything You Need to Know About Staying With Us

Where is Kaduhithlu Resort located?

Kaduhithlu Resort is located near Sakleshpur, surrounded by lush coffee plantations and scenic hills in the Western Ghats.

What makes Kaduhithlu Resort unique?

Our resort offers a peaceful plantation stay experience combined with comfortable cottages, natural surroundings, and warm hospitality.

What types of cottages are available?

We offer cottages suitable for double, triple, and quadruple sharing, ideal for couples, families, and groups.

Are meals provided during the stay?

Yes, we serve simple and delicious home-style meals prepared with care for our guests.

What activities can guests enjoy at the resort?

Guests can enjoy trekking, stream walking, indoor and outdoor games, swimming, and evening campfires

Is the resort suitable for family vacations?

Yes, Kaduhithlu Resort is perfect for family getaways, group trips, and romantic retreats.

Do you offer parking facilities?

Yes, secure parking is available for all guests staying at the resort.

Is the resort surrounded by nature?

Yes, the resort is set amidst coffee plantations, greenery, and peaceful hill views, offering a true nature retreat.
